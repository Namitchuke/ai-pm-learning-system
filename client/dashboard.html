Welcome to AI PM Learning System

Your 24-week roadmap to becoming a Product Manager

This system is your personal study companion. It tracks your progress across 6 phases, tests your knowledge, and prepares you for PM interviews.

Let's take a quick tour of what each section does.

Today's Focus

Your daily study hub

Shows your current week, day, and phase at a glance. This is where you'll spend most of your time.

  • Weekly Checklist — Track items for the current week. Each has a "What this means" explainer and a "Done when" criteria.
  • Weekly Metrics — Use +/- buttons to log cases solved, chapters read, and mock interviews done. Click the number to set it directly.
  • Knowledge Quizzes — Test yourself on each week's concepts. Wrong answers trigger targeted study resources.

Full Overview

See the big picture

A comprehensive view of your entire 24-week journey.

  • Roadmap Progress — Overall completion percentage across all phases and weeks.
  • Skill Depth Tracker — Take quizzes at 5 levels (Intern to Expert) for each core PM and AI skill.
  • Interview Bank — 20 curated behavioral questions with hints to practice.
  • Decision Logs — Record your PM decisions from case studies to build a thinking portfolio.

Dashboard

Track and celebrate progress

Your stats at a glance with visual progress tracking.

  • Achievements — 8 badges that unlock as you hit milestones (first case, first mock, phase completion, etc.)
  • Weekly Grid — See completion % for all 24 weeks in a visual grid.
  • Phase Cards — Expand each phase to see week-by-week breakdown.
  • Lifetime Metrics — Total cases, applications, and mock interviews tracked.

Mock Interviews & Case Studies

Practice for real interviews

151 mock interview questions and 20+ case studies from top companies.

  • Filter by company, category, difficulty, role, and completion status.
  • Checkboxes to mark questions as practiced — tracked in your progress.
  • Hints — Click any question to reveal a structured approach.
  • Books section — 12 recommended PM resources with links at the bottom of Case Studies.

Tip: Use the sidebar (☰ menu) for navigation, export/import, and logout.
